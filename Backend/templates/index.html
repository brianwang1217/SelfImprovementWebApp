Your Personal Messenger

Whenever you are bored, curious, or just lonely, SendMeJunk allows you to send yourself a message of all the latest news, discoveries, and other junk. It contains a database filled with a plethera of online articles, all of which you can choose from. Read from a collection of articles, pick from a variety of sources, subscribe to your favorite newsletter. SendMeJunk is your one stop shop for all the spam in the world.

SendMeJunk can also be your personal reminder. Set your own message, be it a motvational speech or just a simple reminder, and SendMeJunk can send it to you whenever you like.

What SendMeJunk Can Do For You

Ever wake up feeling down, upset at the world for absolutely no reason. SendMeJunk can help out by sending you a compliment every morning that will put a smile on your face!

Ever miss a class because you are so caught up in a intense game of checkers with your friends, don't worry, SendMeJunk can remind you when you have your classes so you won't miss one ever again!
