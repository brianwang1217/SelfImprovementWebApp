About SendMeJunk

SendMeJunk is much more than just a website; it is a revolutionary system that simplifies the user's life through the use of reminders and messages.

SendMeJunk is perfect for people of all ages. It is also perfect for anyone that wants improve their quality of life dramastically in a short period of time.

Created in 2017, SendMeJunk is rapidly becoming the next big thing. Some have even began to make comparisons in its applicability to Google.

About The SendMeJunk Team

SendMeJunk is a group project created by students at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. The project was created as a requirement for CS196, "the best freshman honors supplement course on the planet!"

The goal of this project was to create a website that would allow for essential, as well inessential, messages to be sent to the user based on preferences the user filled out. Tell us how we did!
